Welcome to Smartyogi

In today’s fast-paced world, many people practice yoga at home without proper guidance, often leading to incorrect postures that can cause injury or reduce the effectiveness of the practice. AI Yoga is designed to solve this problem by providing real-time posture detection using computer vision. With just your device’s camera, our intelligent system monitors your yoga poses and offers instant feedback and corrective instructions — helping you practice safely, improve your form, and get the most out of every session, all without the need for a physical trainer.
